Taking the first step can feel difficult, but getting help doesn't have to be. We'll guide you through every stage of your care in a safe and supportive environment.

● STEP – 01

Book Your Appointment

FAQ's →



How do I book an appointment at Safe Space Mind Clinic?
You can schedule an appointment by calling +91 9650719719. Our team will help you choose a suitable consultation date and time.
What conditions do you treat?
We provide care for depression, anxiety, OCD, bipolar disorder, schizophrenia, addiction-related concerns, epilepsy, migraines, and memory-related issues.
Will my consultation and treatment remain confidential?
Yes. All consultations, medical records, and discussions are handled with complete privacy and confidentiality.
Do I need a follow-up appointment after my first visit?
In most cases, follow-up visits are recommended to monitor progress, adjust treatment if needed, and support long-term recovery.
