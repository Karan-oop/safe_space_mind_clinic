Frequently Asked Questions

Your questions answered about our medical services

1. How can I book an appointment with a doctor? −

We provide a wide range of services including general checkups, cardiology, pediatrics, orthopedics, dermatology, and diagnostic tests.

2. Do you provide emergency medical services? +

Yes, we provide 24/7 emergency care and ambulance support for patients needing urgent medical attention.

3. What medical services do you provide? +

Our clinic offers general practice, clinical counseling, addiction detox support, psychiatric evaluations, and full neurological diagnostics.

4. How early should I arrive for my appointment? +

We recommend arriving 10-15 minutes prior to your scheduled consultation to complete any basic registration paperwork.

5. What should I bring to my medical appointment? +

Please bring a valid photo ID, past medical reports, a list of active medications, and insurance documentation if applicable.
